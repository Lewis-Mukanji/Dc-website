Usage

VIZZ (aceclidine ophthalmic solution) 1.44% is a prescription eye drop used to treat age-related blurry near vision (presbyopia) in adults.

Adverse Reactions

The most common reported adverse reactions were instillation site irritation (20%), dim vision (16%), and headache (13%). Most adverse reactions were mild, transient, and self-resolving.

Important Safety Information

  • Do not use VIZZ if you are allergic to any of the ingredients.
  • Do not allow the vial tip to touch the eye or any surfaces.
  • Remove contact lenses before use; reinsert after 10 minutes.
  • If using more than one topical eye medication, administer at least 5 minutes apart.
  • Do not drive or operate machinery if vision is not clear after use.
  • Seek immediate medical care if you experience sudden onset of flashing lights, floaters, or vision loss.

To report suspected adverse reactions: contact LENZ Therapeutics at 1-888-711-LENZ or FDA at 1-800-FDA-1088.
