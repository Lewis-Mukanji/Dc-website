COMMUNITY THAT MATTERS

What is Fusion?

Fusion groups are location-based small groups where believers gather during the week to connect, grow, and serve together. These aren't just meetings — they're families where authentic relationships are built, faith is strengthened, and life is done together.

In a Fusion group, you'll find people from your neighbourhood who are walking the same journey of faith. Here, nobody is a stranger — everyone matters, everyone belongs, and everyone contributes to the growth of others.

WHY JOIN

The Power of
Small Groups

💬

Authentic Relationships

Build deep, meaningful friendships with people who genuinely care about your journey and celebrate your victories.

📖

Biblical Growth

Dive deeper into God's Word through discussion, application, and mutual learning in a safe environment.

🙏

Prayer Support

Experience the power of united prayer as your group intercedes for each other's needs and breakthroughs.

🎯

Personal Accountability

Grow stronger through accountability relationships that help you stay on track with your spiritual goals.

🤝

Practical Care

Find support during life's challenges — from meals during illness to help with moving or childcare.

🌟

Discover Your Gifts

In a small group setting, you'll have opportunities to serve, lead, and discover your spiritual gifts.

YOUR FIRST VISIT

What to Expect
at Fusion

6:30 PM

Welcome & Fellowship

Arrive to warm hospitality. Enjoy light refreshments, catch up with friends, and meet new members. This is your time to unwind from the day.

7:00 PM

Worship & Prayer

Begin with worship songs and a time of corporate prayer. We invite God's presence and share prayer requests.

7:20 PM

Bible Study

Engage in interactive Bible study, discussing the sermon from Sunday or a specific topic. Everyone's voice matters as we learn together.

8:00 PM

Application & Closing

Discuss how to apply what we've learned to daily life. Close with prayer and plan for service or social activities.

8:30 PM

Fellowship Continues

Many stay to chat, laugh, and build relationships. Some of the best conversations happen after the "official" meeting ends!
