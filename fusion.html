COMMUNITY THAT MATTERS

What is Fusion?

Fusion groups are location-based small groups where believers gather during the week to connect, grow, and serve together. These aren't just meetings ‚Äì they're families where authentic relationships are built, faith is strengthened, and life is done together.

In a Fusion group, you'll find people from your neighborhood who are walking the same journey of faith. Here, nobody is a stranger ‚Äì everyone matters, everyone belongs, and everyone contributes to the growth of others.

YOUR FIRST VISIT

What to Expect at Fusion

A typical evening in a Fusion group

6:30 PM

Welcome & Fellowship

Arrive to warm hospitality. Enjoy light refreshments, catch up with friends, and meet new members. This is your time to unwind from the day.

7:00 PM

Worship & Prayer

Begin with worship songs and a time of corporate prayer. We invite God's presence and share prayer requests.

7:20 PM

Bible Study

Engage in interactive Bible study, discussing the sermon from Sunday or a specific topic. Everyone's voice matters as we learn together.

8:00 PM

Application & Closing

Discuss how to apply what we've learned to daily life. Close with prayer and plan for service opportunities or social activities.

8:30 PM

Fellowship Continues

Many stay to chat, laugh, and build relationships. Some of the best conversations happen after the "official" meeting ends!
