THE GOOD NEWS

The Gospel Message

Understanding God's plan of salvation

1

God Loves You

You are not an accident. God created you with purpose and loves you unconditionally. His desire is to have a personal relationship with you and for you to experience abundant life.

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." - John 3:16
God's Love Image
2

We Are All Sinners

Sin has separated humanity from God. We've all fallen short of God's perfect standard, and our sin creates a barrier between us and our Creator. This separation results in spiritual death and eternal consequences.

"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God." - Romans 3:23
Sin Separation Image
3

Jesus is the Solution

God didn't leave us in our sin. He sent Jesus Christ, His only Son, to bridge the gap. Jesus lived a perfect life, died on the cross for our sins, and rose again on the third day, conquering death and offering us forgiveness and new life.

"But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us." - Romans 5:8
Jesus Cross Image
4

Receive and Believe

Salvation is a free gift that cannot be earned. To receive it, you must believe in Jesus Christ as your Lord and Savior, confess your sins, and invite Him into your life. This is not just head knowledge ‚Äì it's a heart transformation.

"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." - Romans 10:9
Salvation Image
5

New Life Begins

When you accept Christ, you become a new creation. Old things pass away, and everything becomes new. You receive the Holy Spirit, eternal life, and the privilege of calling God your Father. Your journey of faith has just begun!

"Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!" - 2 Corinthians 5:17
New Life Image

Ready to Accept Christ?

If you want to give your life to Jesus today, we'd love to pray with you and help you take this life-changing step.
