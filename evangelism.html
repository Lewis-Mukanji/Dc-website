THE GOSPEL MESSAGE

Steps to
Receiving Christ

The simple truth that can change your life forever.

1

Recognise Your Need for God

We all have sinned and fallen short of God's perfect standard. Sin separates us from God and leads to spiritual death. No matter how good we try to be, we cannot save ourselves through our own efforts.

"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God."

Romans 3:23

"For the wages of sin is death, but the gift of God is eternal life in Christ Jesus our Lord."

Romans 6:23
2

Believe in Jesus Christ

God loved us so much that He sent His only Son to die on the cross for our sins. Jesus paid the price we couldn't pay. He died in our place, rose again on the third day, defeating sin and death forever.

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

John 3:16
3

Confess and Receive Him

Salvation is a free gift received by faith. When you believe in your heart that Jesus is Lord and confess Him with your mouth, you will be saved. It's not about being perfect — it's about accepting God's grace.

"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved."

Romans 10:9
4

Have Assurance of Salvation

When you receive Christ, you become a new creation. Your sins are forgiven, and you have eternal life. God's Spirit lives in you, and nothing can separate you from His love. You are now a child of God!

"Therefore, if anyone is in Christ, the new creation has come: The old has gone, the new is here!"

2 Corinthians 5:17
TAKE THE STEP

Ready to Make the Most Important Decision of Your Life?

If you believe in your heart that Jesus Christ is Lord and want to surrender your life to Him, we invite you to take the next step. Today is the day of salvation!
