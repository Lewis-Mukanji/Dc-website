The simple truth that can change your life forever

Recognize Your Need for God

We all have sinned and fallen short of God's perfect standard. Sin separates us from God and leads to spiritual death. No matter how good we try to be, we cannot save ourselves through our own efforts or good works.

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." ‚Äî John 3:16
