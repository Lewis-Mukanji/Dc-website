A Place for Everyone

Whether you're new to faith or just looking for a church home, we're thrilled you're considering Destiny Chapel. We're a community of real people pursuing an authentic relationship with God — and we'd love for you to be part of our family.

Come as you are. You don't need to have it all figured out. Just bring an open heart and a willingness to encounter God's love.

YOUR FIRST VISIT

What to Expect

🚗

Easy Parking

We have ample parking right at the church. Our parking attendants will guide you. Arrive 15 minutes early for the best spots.

👋

Warm Welcome

Our welcome team will greet you with a smile! They can answer questions, show you around, and help you feel at home.

👶

Kids Programs

We offer age-appropriate programs for children during service. Our kids ministry is safe, fun, and designed to help children encounter God's love.

🎵

Engaging Worship

Our worship is contemporary and Spirit-led. Feel free to worship however feels comfortable — stand, sit, raise your hands, or simply listen.

📖

Practical Teaching

Expect biblical teaching that's relevant to real life. Our pastors present God's Word in ways that are easy to understand and apply.

☕

Connect Time

After service, join us for coffee and snacks. A great time to meet people, ask questions, and start building friendships.

QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked
Questions

What should I wear?

▾
Come as you are! We have people in everything from jeans to suits. The important thing is that you're comfortable and your heart is open to worship.

How long is the service?

▾
Our Sunday service typically runs about 2.5–3 hours, but you're welcome to arrive late or leave early if needed. We understand life happens!

Is childcare available?

▾
Yes! We have age-appropriate programmes for children from nursery through elementary. All volunteers are background-checked and trained in child safety.

Will I be asked to give money?

▾
We do take an offering during service, but giving is completely voluntary. As a first-time guest, please don't feel any pressure to give. Just focus on experiencing God's presence.

Can I bring my Bible?

▾
Absolutely! We encourage it. The sermon verses are also displayed on screens so you can follow along even if you don't have one.

How do I become a member?

▾
We offer membership classes throughout the year covering our beliefs, values, and what it means to be part of the Destiny Chapel family. Talk to any pastor or fill out the contact form!
