QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

Come as you are! We have people in everything from jeans to suits. The important thing is that you're comfortable and your heart is open to worship.

How long is the service?

Our Sunday service typically runs about 2.5-3 hours, but you're welcome to arrive late or leave early if needed. We understand life happens!

Is childcare available?

Yes! We have age-appropriate programs for children from nursery through elementary. All volunteers are background-checked and trained in child safety.

Will I be asked to give money?

We do take an offering during service, but giving is completely voluntary. As a first-time guest, please don't feel any pressure to give. Just focus on experiencing God's presence.

Can I bring my Bible?

Absolutely! We encourage it. The sermon verses are also displayed on screens so you can follow along even if you don't have a Bible with you.

How do I become a member?

We offer membership classes throughout the year. These classes cover our beliefs, values, and what it means to be part of the Destiny Chapel family. Talk to any pastor or fill out the contact form!
