Our Story

A Journey of Faith, Hope, and Transformation

WHO WE ARE

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a place where people could encounter God's presence, discover their purpose, and live out their destiny.

Today, we stand as a testament to God's faithfulness, growing from humble beginnings into a thriving community that reaches across neighborhoods, nations, and generations. Every testimony, every life transformed, and every soul saved reminds us of why we do what we do.

OUR JOURNEY

The Destiny Chapel Story

Tracing our path from humble beginnings to a thriving community of faith

2005

The Vision is Born

A small group of passionate believers gathered in a living room, united by a vision to establish a church that would transform lives and impact communities. With nothing but faith and determination, Destiny Chapel was born.

2008

First Permanent Location

God blessed us with our first permanent facility in Ngei. What started as 30 members quickly grew to over 150 as the community witnessed the power of genuine worship and biblical teaching.

2012

Ministry Expansion

We launched our Youth, Children's, Men's, and Women's ministries, recognizing the importance of targeted discipleship for every demographic. Our community outreach programs also began, serving hundreds of families in need.

2015

Missions Movement

Our missions department was established, sending teams to unreached areas both locally and internationally. We partnered with organizations to bring the gospel to remote villages and urban centers alike.

2018

Fusion Groups Launch

Understanding the power of community, we introduced Fusion cell groups ‚Äì small, location-based gatherings that foster deeper relationships and spiritual growth. These groups became the heartbeat of our church.

2020

Food Bank Initiative

During challenging times, we opened the Destiny Chapel Food Bank, demonstrating Christ's love through practical action. To date, we've served over 500 families and continue to be a beacon of hope in our community.

2026

Looking Forward

Today, with over 1,000 active members and growing, we continue to pursue our mission with renewed passion. Our vision remains clear: to build lives, transform communities, and impact the world for Christ.

Our Vision

"To be a transformational church that raises disciples who live out their God-given destiny, impacting communities and nations through the power of the Gospel, authentic worship, and sacrificial service."

OUR MISSION

Why We Exist

Our Mission

To lead people into a life-changing relationship with Jesus Christ through dynamic worship, relevant biblical teaching, and intentional discipleship.

We are committed to equipping every believer to discover and fulfill their divine purpose, serving as ambassadors of Christ in their homes, workplaces, and communities. Through evangelism, missions, and compassionate outreach, we extend God's love to the lost, the hurting, and the marginalized.
